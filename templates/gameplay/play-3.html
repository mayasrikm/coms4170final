HOW TO PLAY

If a face card is placed: the next player must place a face card to continue the game. The number of chances they have to play a face card is determined by the initial face card:

  • Ace - 4 Cards
  • King - 3 Cards
  • Queen - 2 Cards
  • Jack - 1 Card
