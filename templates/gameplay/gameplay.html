WHAT IS EGYPTIAN RAT SLAP?

Egyptian Rat Slap is a card game played by at least 2 players using a deck of 52 playing cards. Players take turns flipping cards onto a central pile, and certain card combinations prompt players to slap the pile to win cards. The first player to collect all the cards wins.
